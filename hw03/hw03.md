**Q1** Get the maximum and minimum of GDP per capita for all continents.

``` r
out<-gapminder%>%
  group_by(continent)%>%
  summarise(max_gdpPercap=max(gdpPercap), min_gdpPercap=min(gdpPercap))
knitr::kable(out)
```
